The MATSim Association now has a dedicated online signup system for memberships.

The new flow covers:

- individual, small-team, and large-team memberships,
- online payment through Payrexx or bank-transfer invoicing,
- immediate invoice generation,
- integrated data-protection information for applicants.

If you want to support the long-term maintenance and development of MATSim, you can now sign up directly via the new [membership page](/association/membership/).

You can find more background on the Association on the [association page](/association/) and the current data-protection notice on the [GDPR page](/association/gdpr/).
